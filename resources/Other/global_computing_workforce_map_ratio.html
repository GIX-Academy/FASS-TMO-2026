Interactive world choropleth map with three views: computer-using workforce (Tier 1+2+3), computing profession (Tier 3), and Tier 3 as share of all computer-using workers, for approximately 155 countries.

Estimation model: Tier 1 = f(internet penetration, agricultural employment share). Tier 3 = f(GDP/capita band, regional ICT benchmarks). Ratio = Tier 3 % of total employment ÷ Tier 1+2+3 % of total employment. Anchor data: Eurostat 2023–24, IMDA 2024, BLS 2023, ITU 2023–24 (35 countries direct). ~120 additional countries estimated via calibrated model. Confidence: ±5pp high-income, ±8pp upper-mid, ±12pp low-income.
