Interactive world choropleth map estimating computer-using workforce (Tier 1+2+3) and computing profession (Tier 3) as share of employment for approximately 150 countries, derived from ITU internet penetration, agricultural employment share, and GDP per capita proxies, calibrated against 35 directly measured anchor countries.

Estimation model: Tier 1 = f(internet penetration, agricultural employment share, GDP/capita). Tier 3 = f(GDP/capita, services sector share, regional ICT benchmarks). Anchor data: Eurostat 2023–24, IMDA 2024, BLS 2023, ITU 2023–24, national statistical offices (35 countries with direct measurement). ~120 additional countries estimated via calibrated model. Confidence bands: ±5pp for high-income; ±8pp for middle-income; ±12pp for low-income countries.
